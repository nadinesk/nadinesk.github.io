  • 3/10/24: Real Housewives of Miami, S6 E21

    • I watched the third part of the reunion while folding laundry early morning. I got absorbed into the episode but it's been a few days since I watched it!

      The second and third parts of this reunion got much deeper than the first part. Kiki explained more about her past and her background. How can one not be on her side? It was basically tragic when they showed the scene where Lisa says somethign like, "What do you want me to do?! I'm not a therapist!" after Kiki tried to explain to Lisa to basically have some sensitivity to those around you, and some empathy, when she sees non-wealthy people and people in different financial situations than she normally does.

      Nothing made me realize how far removed from out of touch these women are than the discussion about how Lisa should maybe drive a Toyota instead of one of Lenny's cars. I've only basically owned Corollas! And I was beyond excited when we got the one that I still drive. I found that whole discussion of the cars to be so funny and yet so crazy. It was funny until Lisa mockingly drove her nanny/maid's car to an event to prove some kind of point - which was no point because it was her employees' car. No matter how much Guerdy, especially, tries to give Lisa some perspective, Lisa cannot get it.

      I have watched Guerdy all season since she got diagnosed with breast cancer, try to tell these women, begging them, to get some perspective. Things are good with them overall, despite minor things and they maybe shouldn't fight so much (but would that be best for the viewers? Questionable). I think she gets through to some of them, maybe who are closer to illness, like Nicole and Julia via Martina's illness and treatment. But for the rest of them, it's like there is a thick wall she cannot bore through no matter how she tells them, no matter how she shows them what she is experiencing and how difficult it is. It's hard to teach people to have perspective. They would enjoy what they have more if they did, and that is what I think Guerdy wants to tell them. But they are not there to absorb it beyond anything but maybe like 2%.

      And how can you blame them? Because they don't need to get it right now. And that's why I think the whole thing with Larsa's odd response to Guerdy telling her she has breast cancer (What? How? How? How do you know? Are you sure?) is somewhat forgivable. I give her the benefit of the doubt based on this reasoning, but if it's anything else then it's completely awful: I think she is in the mode of creating a reality TV show. She films scenes and she knows she's creating a scene. When Guerdy told her she has cancer, she was stunned because she had a script in her mind that the would talk about Guerdy calling her fake. Guerdy went off script. All Larsa could think about was: Oh no! Is this scene now going to make me look bad?! Is Guerdy trying to make me look bad?! She automatically then went to the mode of having to defend herself from looking bad in the scene, to the audience. She didn't have time to think through this new scenario and she completely freaked out. That is how I took her reaction. If it's anything else, I don't want to think about that.
